About

Welcome to my website, where I am documenting my journey with learning to code. This site is to serve as a central point to keep track of my progress, and to store all coding projects I have worked on while I'm learning.

Background in coding

My background is in languages and linguistics (I have a degree in German and Spanish Linguistic Studies). I realised that my favourite part of language learning wasn't being able to speak with lots of people (I'm an introvert), or watch foreign series on Netflix (although I do enjoy that!). For me, the best part was always the learning process itself: finding out the grammar rules and applying them, using logic to solve problems. And my favourite part of my university course was syntax. This realisation led to me thinking that coding might be up my street.

I decided to have a go to see what I thought. The first coding language I dabbled in was Python. I then paid for a short course with SheCodes to learn the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, as many sources seemed to cite those languages as the best starting point. See my SheCodes page and certificate here.

Next I signed up for the full membership with CodeCademy and am now working my way through the Front-End Engineer pathway. I'm self-employed so I can fit my coding study around my work responsibilities, which is great. I'm looking forward to learning more and sharing my practice projects here!
